About Us

We manufacture and supply electric cars in Australia, while minimizing the environmental impact, reducing cost and providing futuristic design.

Join the future of sustainable transportation!

Vision

"To lead the electric-vehicles sales market in Australia, providing customers with a product that offers exceptional value for their daily mode of transportation."

Mission

"To bridge the gap between demand and supply in Australia’s electric vehicles."

According to the research done by Transportation of Australia, Only 25% market is occupied by electric vehicles. We are here to broaden the usage of electric vehicles in an affordable price.

  • Low rates on vehicles
  • Eco-friendly vehicle with lesser carbon emissions
  • Battery capacity that can last upto 450Km in single charge

We aim to be considered as a go-to vehicle, that serves as a primary choice for every customer. Our vehicles have full potential to compete with gas-powered vehicles, in terms of design, usability, and cost.

Features

Let’s discuss some mind-blowing features of our electric vehicle that distinguishes us from other cars.

Longer Journies

Our vehicle can easily travel 300+ kilometer in single charge. Our batteries are awarded as best to be used in vehicles

Smartphone Access

Smartphones can be used to navigate, monitor and access the features of the vehicle. Also you can use smartphone to locate charging stations near you

EV-Specific Displays

The dashboard display will indicate if you are driving in an eco-friendly manner enabling you to conserve battery power and maximise the range available from the remaining charge

Quiet And Smooth

We offer a considerably quieter and overall smoother ride than conventional gas-based cars. With no gears to shift, it makes driving hassle-free

Parking Assistance

Combining reversing (rear-facing) cameras, 360-degree camera, and sensors help you assist in car parking

Interior and Leg-space

We provide a premium interior, and most spacious seats and roomy luggage compartments for comfortable seating and more storage.
